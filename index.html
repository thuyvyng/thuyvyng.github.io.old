Experience



Currently, I'm a Software Developer at Oracle on the OHAI Owls team. I was previously on the Shepherd Deployment Automation Team where I interned in Summer 2020 and rejoined full-time in July 2021. During my internship I created SheepDog, a Slack application to immediately notify users about their automated deployment's status updates.

In Spring 2021, I volunteered with Devlop For Good and was partnered with ideas42 to help implement Vergil: a web app designed to help probationers and parolees navigate their case plan responsibilities within their communities.

I completed my undergrad in Computer Science from Oregon State University with a minor in Psych and a focus in Human Computer Interaction. At Oregon State, I was an Undergraduate Developer at TAHMO under Dr. Tom Dietterich, which aims to create a network of weather stations in Africa and utilize machine learning algorithms to provide insight on agricultural, climate monitoring, and hydro-meteorological applications. I was also a Research Assistant in the Data Interaction and Visualization Lab lead by Dr. Minsuk Kahng. This lab focuses on creating data visualization tools to help people learn, explore, and understand machine-learning models.

I was also an IMPACT Lead Volunteer, Society of Women Engineers Ambassador, Project X Volunteer, Research Assistant in the CARVE Lab, URSA Engage Research Assistant, Pre-College Programs Ambassador and Tour Guide, Honors College Web Editor, and College of Engineering Peer Tutor for CS & Math.
