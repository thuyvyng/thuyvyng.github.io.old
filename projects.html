Using Virtual Reality to Model and Describe Environmental Impacts

I was a 2018-2019 URSA Enagage scholarship recipient at Oregon State. This allowed me to recieve funding for completing a research project under mentoship of an OSU faculty member. Dr. Raffaele De Amicis, helped me construct and implement my project. I presented my research at the Oregon State University Celebrating Undergraduate Showcase in May 2019.

This projected aimed to use virtual reality technology to explore how technology can be used to conserve environemtal resources. I created an interactiv and visual model that would show the user changes to their environment after climate change. This project hopes to be a useful tool to determine the possible effects of climate changes on an environment.
